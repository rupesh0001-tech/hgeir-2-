Government Schemes · Live Subsidies

Who Walks In

Institutional buyers with budgets, not browsers.

We've designed HGER to attract decision-makers from four high-impact institutional segments — every one of them sitting on procurement budgets and ESG mandates this fiscal year.

Schools & Colleges

28% of footfall

1,200+ schools and 80+ colleges across Haryana looking for rooftop solar, energy education programs, and clean-tech partnerships under the PM Surya Ghar scheme.

Hospitals & Healthcare

22% of footfall

Government hospitals, private clinics, and PHCs seeking 24×7 solar backup, oxygen plant power, and reliable clean-energy systems for critical care infrastructure.

Govt. Bodies & Panchayats

26% of footfall

Block offices, municipal bodies, and 6,000+ Gram Panchayats with mandated procurement for solar streetlights, EV fleets, water pumps, and public-building retrofits.

Hotels & Commercial

24% of footfall

Hotels, resorts, malls, and large commercial buildings under ESG compliance pressure — high-ticket buyers for solar, energy storage, EV charging, and efficiency retrofits.
