The 10 Cities

A city-by-city strategy for total reach.

Every district selected for its unique buyer profile — from cement plants in Charkhi Dadri to Murthal hospitality in Sonipat, basmati exporters in Karnal to plywood SMEs in Yamunanagar. No overlap. No waste.

What Happens at Every City

A complete activation, not just a booth.

Each HGER stop is a 3-day, fully-produced ground event. Here's what every sponsor and visitor experiences.

Custom-built stalls

Premium branded, weatherproof stalls — fresh setup in every city, designed for premium product showcasing.

Govt. enrolment desks

HAREDA + DISCOM officers helping visitors apply for PM Surya Ghar, PM-KUSUM subsidies on the spot.

Live product demos

Real solar panels, EV test rides, smart-home setups — visitors touch, feel, and compare side-by-side.

Press & lead capture

Local Haryanvi-language press, digital lead scanning kiosks, and visitor demographic tracking for sponsors.
