THE GRAND FINALE · CHANDIGARH · 15 MAY 2026

The HGER Summit
& Awards Night.

An evening that closes 3 months of journey — uniting policymakers, industry leaders, sponsors, dealers, and changemakers shaping Haryana's renewable energy future. With awards across 6 prestigious categories and Hon'ble Sh. Anil Vij as Chief Guest.

AV
CHIEF GUEST · 2026
Chief Guest of Honour

Sh. Anil Vij

Hon'ble Minister
Power, Energy & Transport, Haryana

A veteran statesman and one of Haryana's most respected political voices, Sh. Anil Vij brings decades of public-service leadership and deep familiarity with the state's energy and infrastructure agenda. As Chief Guest of the HGER 2026 Summit & Awards Night, he will deliver the keynote address, formally recognise the award winners across both Exhibitor and Visitor tracks, and announce HGER 2027.

Evening Agenda · 15 May 2026

The full programme.

A carefully curated 4-hour evening — combining business, recognition, and celebration.

5:30 PM

Registration & Networking Welcome

Curated welcome zone, sponsor showcase, networking with district commissioners, university heads, and CXOs.

6:30 PM

Opening Ceremony · Lamp Lighting

Hon'ble Minister Sh. Anil Vij, senior officials from HAREDA, DHBVN, UHBVN, and the HGER leadership inaugurate the evening.

7:00 PM

Keynote · Hon'ble Sh. Anil Vij

Address on Haryana's renewable energy roadmap, state policy direction, and the role of grassroots adoption in the energy transition.

7:30 PM

Expert Panel · The Future of Green Haryana

30-minute panel discussion with industry leaders, HAREDA, and senior policymakers on solar, EV, and decentralised renewable adoption.

8:15 PM

Track I · Exhibitor Awards Presentation

Renewable Energy Brand of the Year, Most Innovative Green Technology, Sustainability Leadership Award — presented by the Hon'ble Minister.

8:45 PM

Track II · Visitor Awards Presentation

Sustainable Farmer, Greenest Institution, Renewable Champion — recognising real changemakers from across the 10 cities.

9:15 PM

HGER 2027 Announcement

The next chapter — expanded route, multi-state vision, and sponsor pre-bookings open.

9:30 PM

Gala Dinner & Networking

Premium curated dinner, sponsor-buyer meets, and a closing toast to the HGER 2026 journey.

Awards · 6 Prestigious Categories

Honouring excellence from every side of the green energy story.

Two carefully designed tracks — one for industry leaders, one for grassroots champions. Awards presented on-stage by the Hon'ble Minister with state-level media coverage.

Track I

Exhibitor Awards

Recognising industry leaders & sponsor excellence across the 10-city journey.

01
Renewable Energy Brand of the Year

For the brand delivering highest visitor impact, lead conversion, and brand recall.

02
Most Innovative Green Technology

For breakthrough product, system, or solution showcased during HGER 2026.

03
Sustainability Leadership Award

For demonstrable ESG commitment, social impact, and long-term renewable vision.

Track II

Visitor Awards

Celebrating real adopters and champions of green energy across Haryana.

04
Sustainable Farmer of Haryana

For a farmer leading solar/biofuel adoption, PM-KUSUM enrolment, or agri-energy innovation.

05
Greenest Institution

School, hospital, or college with the most impactful renewable energy adoption story.

06
Renewable Champion

An exceptional household, panchayat, or SME leading green energy adoption locally.
